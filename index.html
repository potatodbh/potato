What the stats mean

GDP = total output (growth).
Unemp = joblessness (higher is worse).
Inflation = CPI change (too high hurts).
Inequality = 0–10 scale (higher means less equal).

Prosperity Score rewards growth + stability.

How to Play — EconNation
Goal
Race to the Finish tile. To win, you must reach the finish and have Prosperity Score ≥ 15.
Your Turn
  1. Click Roll Dice
  2. Move to a tile
  3. Answer the turn card (A/B)
  4. Stats update immediately (sometimes with delayed effects)
Tile Types
Decision • Quick Quiz • Policy • Market • Trade • Bonus • Risk • Start • Finish
Course connection (CIE3M)
Scarcity and opportunity cost, supply and demand, price controls, GDP/CPI, business cycle, fiscal vs monetary policy, budgeting and investing, and trade.
Playtime
60 tiles with choices is typically ~20 minutes for 2–4 players.
Tip
Don’t chase GDP only. High unemployment, inflation, or inequality can wreck Prosperity.
